"A great start, but a terrible finish"

When I went to Carl's Jr. I got the western bacon-cheeseburger with fries and a medium pop. The cost for my meal was $11 dollars but that was completely acceptable because everything was gigantic so you got more then enough for th portion size,in fact you got to much because once you finish the meal you will feel bloated. Once I got my food saw the presentation was nice because you got a nice basket for your fries which is pretty uncommon for fast/fan food. The first half of the burger was good but then the second half of my burger was terrible because the bread was dripping underneath with sauce,and that sauce became so powerful and disgusting I actually had to put the burger down and stop eating it, the fires weren't anything special either but they weren't bad. Because of to much food I was given and the terrible burger I have to give this meal a 2 out of 5 burgers.Visit the Carls Jr. Website here!
