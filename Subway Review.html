"A unique review for a unique restaurant"

At subway I got the foot long meatball sub with chips and a pop. The pricing is pretty good because you get a foot long sandwich with unlimited toppings, chips, and a medium pop for $11. The portion size is a bit much because after I finished my sandwich I was completely full but luckily with chips you can carry it, or put it in your bag and save it for later. The taste was good but its definitely not amazing or anything. The presentation was obviously messy because its a meatball sub. In all the sandwich had no flaws but the taste could use some work so I've decided to give this meal a 4 out 5 burgers.Look at how subway made their own website!
