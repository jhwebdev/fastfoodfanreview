"A cheap price for a solid meal"

When I went to dairy queen I decided to get the $7 meal deal and it came with a bacon-cheeseburger with a medium pop,fires, and a chocolate sundae. The price is honestly the best price for any fast food/fan food restaurant. When i got this meal I was blown away by the presentation due to its poor pricing, so I was real excited to taste the burger and when I took my first bite I was extremely disappointed. I was disappointed because the burger was burned and the sauce was only on half the burger but luckily the sundae and the fries made up for the terrible taste of the burger. After I finished my meal I was perfectly filled up, not to full or hungry, so bonus points for that. In all the pricing, presentation, the portion-size, the sundae, and the fires made up the taste for the burger, so I have to give this meal a 4 out of 5 burgers.
